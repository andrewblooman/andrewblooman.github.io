Hello — I'm Andrew.

I'm a Cloud Security Engineer with over seven years' experience securing critical platforms across Financial Services and Fintech. I currently lead the Platform Security team at IG Group, where my focus is on building secure-by-default cloud foundations that engineering teams genuinely want to use.

My career began deep in the SOC — running SIEMs, hunting threats, and keeping the lights on at firms like Mizuho Bank and Inmarsat. Over time my work shifted upstream: from detecting issues to designing systems where those issues are far less likely to occur. Today my time is split between architecture, automation, and helping teams ship safely on AWS and Kubernetes.

I'm a strong believer that the best security is invisible — it shows up as a reliable platform, a clear contract, and a team that can move fast without breaking things. I write about that craft on Medium, hold a stack of cloud and security certifications, and was recognised as a Wiz MVP for community contribution.
